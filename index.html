I am currently pursuing a combined Master's-PhD program in Information and Communication Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (from 2017 to now).

Research

My research interests lie in the fields of deep learning, image quality assessment, and computer vision, etc. I have published papers in relevant journals and conferences, such as IEEE TMM, IEEE TCSVT, ACM MM, Displays, IEEE ICIP, IEEE SMC, IEEE VCIP.
